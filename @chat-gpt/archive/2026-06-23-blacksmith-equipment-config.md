# 2026-06-23 blacksmith equipment config

## Scope
- Added generated equipment/building assets to the Arkini source package.
- Added Blacksmith I as a producer plus its blueprint and craft recipe.
- Added hero equipment items: Axe, Sword, Leather Armor, Iron Armor.
- Re-priced Heroes Guild expedition inputs around crafted gear.

## New production chain
Town Hall IV can now issue `Blacksmith I Blueprint`.

`Blacksmith I` products:
- Axe: 1 Iron Ingot + 1 Plank + 1 Leather, 15s
- Sword: 2 Iron Ingots + 1 Coal + 1 Leather + 1 Water, 25s
- Leather Armor: 3 Leather + 1 Plank + 1 Coin, 30s
- Iron Armor: 4 Iron Ingots + 2 Leather + 2 Coal + 1 Water, 45s

The sword is intentionally pricier and more precise than the axe: extra iron, coal finishing, leather grip, and water quench.

## Heroes Guild expedition gear
- Chest I: Axe + Bread + Beer
- Chest II: Axe + Leather Armor + Sausage + 2 Coins
- Chest III: Sword + Iron Armor + 2 Wine Glasses + 4 Coins
- Chest IV: 2 Swords + Iron Armor + Wine Barrel + Beer Barrel + 8 Coins

Design note: Axe gates level I/II expeditions; Iron Armor gates level III/IV expeditions.
